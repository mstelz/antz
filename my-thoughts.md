Actions available

Colony Tiles
------------

*   **Food storage**
    
    *   Purpose: can hold X amount of food required to feed the queen (and the colony?)
        
    *   Cost: Leaves and Building materials
        
*   **Nursery**
    
    *   Purpose: to raise an egg through the lifecycle (egg -> larvae -> pupa -> ant)
        
    *   Cost: 
        
    *   Requirements: requires food to be placed on it for the ants to progress in their development
        
    *   Upgrades: 
        
        *   Level 1: may incubate a single egg at a time
            
        *   Level 2: may incubate up to 3 eggs at a time
            
        *   Level 3: may incubate up to 5 eggs at a time
            
*   **Expansion Chamber**
    
    *   Cost:
        
    *   Purpose: Allows for the construction of additional chambers

Event Cards
-----------

#### Rain

*   This will increase the water level, flooding the deepest portion of a colony (maybe 3-4 deep)
    
*   Another rain will increase again another level up
    
*   The rain will wash away, eggs, and food
    

#### Drought

*   Removes all levels of flooding
    

#### Pesticides

*   Much like rain but works from the top down instead of bottom up
    
*   This will also eliminate workers / soldiers and if not careful the queen 
    

#### Ice Cream Cone or other Food Variant

#### Lawn Mower

*   Causes workers and/or soldiers to be potentially swept up
    

#### Frost

*   Slows down movement
    

#### Spider/Bee Swarm/Caterpillar 

*   Spider: hard to kill for food
    
*   Caterpillar: easy to “kill” for food
